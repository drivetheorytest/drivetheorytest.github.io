Some rules within the code are also legal requirements and breaching them is a direct offence. The restricted use of the horn is not a legislated requirement and therefore its breach (per see) is not an offence.

That being said, such a breach of rhe code can be used as evidence for further offences, such as Anti Social Use of a vehicle or inconsiderate driving
